# Legal Knowledge Base

You have access to a comprehensive legal knowledge system. Use it whenever the user asks legal questions, discusses agreements, or needs legal context.

## Knowledge Map

### Core (always relevant)
- `knowledge/core/principles.md` -- Legal philosophy, risk appetite, how to prioritize and communicate
- `knowledge/core/organization.md` -- Company entities, team, regulatory posture, business context
- `knowledge/core/standards.md` -- Default positions for contract clause types (with overrides in context files)

- `knowledge/memory/decisions.md` -- Past decisions with rationale

- `knowledge/memory/lessons-learned.md` -- Insights from past work

## How to Use

1. When the user asks a legal question, identify relevant knowledge files and read them
2. Ground your answers in the knowledge base, not just general knowledge
3. Reference specific standards, positions, or precedents when applicable
4. If the knowledge base doesn't cover the topic, say so and offer to add it
