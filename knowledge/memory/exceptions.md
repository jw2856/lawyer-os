# Exceptions Log

> Record exceptions to standard positions — when and why we deviated from our standards.
> This prevents inconsistency and helps calibrate future negotiations.

## Format

```
### [Date] — [Partner/Vendor] — [Clause Type]
**Standard position**: What our standard says
**Exception granted**: What we agreed to instead
**Why**: Business justification for the exception
**Conditions**: Any conditions or compensating controls
**Precedent risk**: Could this be cited against us? How to handle.
```
