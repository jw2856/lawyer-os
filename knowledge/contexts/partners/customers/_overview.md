## Standard Customer Agreement Considerations

When reviewing a customer agreement, focus on:

1. **Service description** -- Clear scope of what we're providing
2. **SLAs** -- Our uptime and performance commitments
3. **Liability** -- Our exposure for service failures
4. **Data protection** -- How we handle customer and end-user data
5. **IP** -- Protecting our technology while granting appropriate licenses
6. **Integration requirements** -- Technical obligations and timelines
7. **Fees** -- Revenue model, payment terms, minimum commitments
