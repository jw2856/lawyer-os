# Partner Terms Checklist

Checklist specific to strategic partner agreements. Use alongside the general contract review checklist. Customize items based on your organization's partner relationship types (see `contexts/_index.md`).

## Partner-Specific Items

- [ ] **Entity structure**: Correct entity signing per `core/organization.md`?
- [ ] **Coverage**: Geographic scope and corridors clearly defined?
- [ ] **Settlement**: Mechanism clear (BTC/Lightning, USDC, fiat)?
- [ ] **Settlement timing**: Defined with specific targets?
- [ ] **Fund segregation**: Explicitly required?
- [ ] **Non-exclusivity**: Confirmed both ways?
- [ ] **SLA commitments**: Uptime target (99.95% standard)? Latency? Settlement time?
- [ ] **SLA remedies**: Service credits AND termination right for repeated failures?
- [ ] **Refund mechanism**: Denomination and timing clear? Rate determination?
- [ ] **API terms**: Breaking change notification (15 days standard)? Rate limits? Versioning?
- [ ] **KYC/AML allocation**: Clear who performs customer due diligence?
- [ ] **Transaction monitoring**: Responsibility and threshold allocation?
- [ ] **Insurance**: Requirements specified if applicable?
- [ ] **Fee model**: Per-transaction (standard)? Volume thresholds? Minimum commitments?
- [ ] **Fee changes**: Requires consent? Caps on increases? Notice period?
- [ ] **FX provisions**: Rate benchmarks? Spread caps? Methodology disclosed?
- [ ] **Network fee fluctuations**: Who absorbs Bitcoin/crypto network fee spikes?
- [ ] **Regulatory compliance**: Both parties' obligations clear?
- [ ] **Security certifications**: ISO 27001 and SOC 2 Type II required?
- [ ] **Data breach notification**: Timeline (48 hours standard)?

## One-Sided Indemnity Pattern

For every section where your organization must indemnify the partner, verify carve-out for:
- [ ] Partner's negligence, error, or willful misconduct
- [ ] Partner's failure to comply with applicable law or agreement
- [ ] Acts/omissions of partner's network partners or third-party providers
