### Template: Mutual Cap with Carveouts
```
The aggregate liability of each party under or in connection with this
Agreement shall not exceed an amount equal to the total fees paid or
payable by [Customer] to [Provider] during the twelve (12) month period
immediately preceding the event giving rise to such liability.

The foregoing limitation shall not apply to: (a) a party's fraud, gross
negligence, or willful misconduct; (b) breach of confidentiality obligations;
(c) infringement of intellectual property rights; (d) indemnification
obligations; or (e) a party's obligations under applicable data protection
laws.
```

## Indemnification Patterns

### Template: One-Sided Indemnity Carve-Out
When your organization must indemnify a counterparty, add:
```
Notwithstanding the foregoing, [Customer]'s indemnification obligations
under this Section shall not apply to the extent that any Losses arise
from or are attributable to: (i) [Provider]'s negligence, error, or
willful misconduct; (ii) [Provider]'s failure to comply with Applicable
Law or the terms of this Agreement; or (iii) the acts or omissions of
[Provider]'s Network Partners, sub-processors, or Third Party Providers.
```
