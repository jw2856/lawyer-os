# Consumer Protection

## Key US Frameworks
- **FTC Act Section 5**: Prohibits unfair or deceptive acts or practices
- **State consumer protection / UDAP statutes**: Every state has its own (often broader than federal)
- **CFPB**: Consumer Financial Protection Bureau — regulates consumer financial products
- **EFTA / Regulation E**: Electronic fund transfers — error resolution, unauthorized transactions, disclosure
- **TILA / Regulation Z**: Truth in Lending — disclosure of credit terms
- **ESIGN Act**: Electronic signatures and records in commerce

## Key Concepts
- **Unfair practices**: Causes substantial injury, not reasonably avoidable, not outweighed by benefits
- **Deceptive practices**: Material representation or omission likely to mislead reasonable consumers
- **Dark patterns**: UI/UX designed to trick users — increasing regulatory scrutiny
- **Auto-renewal laws**: Many states require specific disclosures and easy cancellation (California ARL)
- **Arbitration clauses**: Enforceable in consumer agreements (per FAA/Supreme Court) but regulatory pressure growing

## Consumer-Facing Terms
- **Terms of service / Terms of use**: Govern the user's relationship with the platform
- **Privacy policy**: Required disclosures about data practices
- **Acceptable use policy**: What users can/cannot do on the platform
- **Cookie policy**: Required in EU/UK, increasingly in US states

## Common Issues
- Adequate disclosure of material terms
- Clear and conspicuous language (not buried in fine print)
- Reasonable cancellation and refund processes
- Compliance with state-specific auto-renewal requirements
- Age restrictions and parental consent (COPPA for under-13)
- Accessibility requirements

## Financial Services Specific
- **Money transmission disclosures**: Fee disclosure, exchange rate transparency, error resolution
- **Remittance transfer rule (Regulation E Subpart B)**: Applies to international money transfers — specific disclosure and error resolution requirements
- **State-specific requirements**: Vary by money transmitter license terms
