# Amendment Drafting Playbook

## When This Applies

When drafting an amendment to an existing agreement — whether to modify commercial terms, add services, update provisions, or address issues that have arisen.

## Required Context

- **Always**: `core/principles.md`, `core/standards.md`
- **Critical**: The original agreement (must read before drafting)
- **Situational**: Partner/vendor context file if it exists

## Process

### Step 1: Understand the Change

- What specifically is being changed and why?
- Is this a business-driven change (new services, pricing) or legal-driven (fixing a gap, updating provisions)?
- Are there multiple changes that should be bundled?
- What's the urgency?

### Step 2: Review the Original Agreement

- Read the original agreement (or last amendment) to understand current terms
- Identify the specific sections being modified
- Check if the agreement has an amendment clause (how amendments must be executed)
- Note the defined terms — the amendment must use consistent definitions

### Step 3: Draft the Amendment

Standard amendment structure:

```
AMENDMENT NO. [X] TO [AGREEMENT NAME]

This Amendment No. [X] ("Amendment") to the [Agreement Name] dated [date]
(the "Agreement") is entered into as of [effective date] ("Amendment
Effective Date") by and between:

[Party 1 full legal name] ("[Short name]")
and
[Party 2 full legal name] ("[Short name]")

RECITALS

WHEREAS, the Parties entered into the Agreement on [date]; and
WHEREAS, the Parties desire to amend the Agreement as set forth herein.

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual promises herein, the Parties
agree as follows:

1. DEFINITIONS
   Capitalized terms used but not defined in this Amendment shall have the
   meanings set forth in the Agreement.

2. AMENDMENTS
   [Section-by-section changes — "Section X of the Agreement is hereby
   amended to read as follows:" or "The following is added as new Section X:"]

3. NO OTHER CHANGES
   Except as expressly modified by this Amendment, the Agreement remains in
   full force and effect. In the event of any conflict between this Amendment
   and the Agreement, this Amendment shall control.

4. COUNTERPARTS
   This Amendment may be executed in counterparts, each of which shall be
   deemed an original.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the Parties have executed this Amendment as of the
Amendment Effective Date.
```

### Step 4: Review the Amendment

- Does it accurately reflect the intended changes?
- Does it use the same defined terms as the original agreement?
- Is the effective date clear?
- Are there any unintended consequences of the changes?
- Does the amendment clause in the original agreement require specific formalities?

## Key Principles

- **Be surgical** — only change what needs to change. Don't open up other provisions for renegotiation.
- **Use the original agreement's language** — maintain consistent defined terms and style.
- **Reference specific sections** — "Section 3.2(a) is hereby amended" not "the liability section is changed."
- **Preserve the rest** — always include a "no other changes" clause.
- **Consider downstream effects** — does changing one provision affect others? (e.g., changing fees might affect liability cap if it's tied to fees paid)
